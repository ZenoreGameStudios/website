About us

David

Born and raised in Zaragoza, from an early age I loved videogames and computers. Despite that, it wasn't until 2 years ago that I started to grow interested on programming and 3D modeling. Thanks to a few online tutorials and some books I read about that, I soon learned to do some small things in these areas. Today, working on Unity, a game engine, I'm beginning to create some games, and I decided to share them through Zenore.

So far, Zenore Game Studios has no more staff, though in a near future we may be able to have a few more enthusiast of the different areas that make up videogame developing, such as programming or 3D modeling, working with us.

There will be a few collaborations on certain things, and those will be mentioned wherever they are relevant.
