CodeBreaker ANDROID v0.1

CodeBreaker ANDROID v0.1

It is our first complete game. It is currently published independently from the traditional app markets such as Google Play, though in a near future we would like to publish it in some of those places. In this game you have to find out a four-digit code which digits do not repeat, and you have several tries. The numbers will change color to indicate whether you got the digit right or not. You will get more points if you use less tries. If you use too many, you might get a negative score.
